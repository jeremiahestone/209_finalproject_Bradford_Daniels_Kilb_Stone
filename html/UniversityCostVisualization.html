Welcome to our website! We help students and parents learn about university costs. With tuition fees going up and student loans becoming a big problem, it's important to know how much college will cost. This can help you choose the right school and plan your finances.

We give you information on how university costs have changed over time and what has caused these changes. We also talk about financial aid and how it can make college more affordable. There are different types of aid you can get, and we'll show you how they can help.

Inflation is when the overall price of things goes up over time, making money worth less. It also affects college costs, so we'll discuss how it impacts the price of education and how to plan for it. We'll explain how to understand the changing value of money and make smart choices for the future. Plus, we'll look at how long it might take you to pay off your student loans based on how much money you make after you graduate.

Our goal is to help you make good choices about college. By understanding the costs, you can plan better for your future and reach your goals without too much money stress. So, whether you're a student or a parent, explore our site to find helpful resources.

Although the sticker price of undergraduate universities has increased drastically in recent decades, the average grant aid offered by these institutions has also increased, leading to an overall decrease in net costs over the past few years (adjusting for inflation). It is important to note that while this additional grant aid has reduced the average net cost of tuition across public and private universities, the cost of room and board has continued to rise, to the point where room and board now makes up the vast majority of the total cost of attending a public university.
